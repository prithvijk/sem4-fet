What is Computer Science?

Computer Science is the study of computers and how they work. It includes programming, algorithms, and data structures.

Data Structures

Data structures are used to store and organize data. Examples include arrays, stacks, and queues.

Algorithms

Algorithms are step-by-step instructions to solve problems. They help make programs efficient and fast.

Conclusion

Computer Science is an important field that helps build software, websites, and applications used in daily life.
